Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

TravelBooker ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including: name, email address, phone number, passport details, payment information, and travel preferences. We also collect information automatically, such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and browsing patterns on our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: process and manage your bookings, send booking confirmations and travel updates, personalise your experience and recommend packages, improve our services and platform, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud and abuse.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with: travel providers necessary to fulfil your booking, payment processors for secure transactions, service partners who assist in platform operations, and authorities when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Booking records are retained for 7 years. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also object to processing or request data portability. To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@travelbooker.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits to protect your information.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact: privacy@travelbooker.com or write to TravelBooker, 45 Glenn Aber Street, Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function. These include session cookies, authentication tokens, and security cookies. These cannot be disabled.

Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our platform by collecting anonymous analytics data. We use these to improve performance and user experience.

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language, currency, and saved searches to personalise your experience.

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness. You may opt out of these cookies.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality. To opt out of analytics cookies, visit our Cookie Preferences centre in your account settings.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services including Google Analytics, Stripe (payments), and Intercom (support chat) which may set their own cookies. Please review their respective privacy policies.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our platform.
